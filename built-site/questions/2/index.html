Flexibility opportunity map

We took the Embedded Capacity Register, and analysed it to understand where market opportunities might exist for provision of flexibility services.

The hexmap below has a number of data layers aggregated to DNO licence areas. The colour scale represents flexibility capacity for each technology, demonstrating where there is potential to procure flexibility of that type. Lighter colours means there is greater capacity in that licence area. Hover over the region for exact figures.
